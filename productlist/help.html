Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add your items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using multiple payment methods.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking info. You can also log in to track your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, as well as UPI and wallets.

How do I return or exchange an item?

You may return items within 30 days. View our Return Policy.
